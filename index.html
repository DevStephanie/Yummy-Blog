Korean Cuisine

Everything you've always wanted to know about Korean Cuisine from someone that loves food!

I'm not expert in Korean food but it's some of my favorite to go out and enjoy! My first introduction to it was Kimchi.

Korean cuisine is known vibrant flavors and diverse array of dishes- truly a delightful culinary experience. A key aspect of Korean food is its emphasis on balance, incorporating a variety of ingredients and cooking techniques. Rice, vegetables, and meats form the foundation of many Korean meals. One iconic Korean dish is kimchi, a fermented side dish made from cabbage or radishes, seasoned with chili peppers, garlic, ginger, and salt. It's a staple in Korean households and known for its health benefits and unique tangy taste. Bulgogi, a popular Korean barbecue dish, features thinly sliced marinated beef or pork grilled to perfection.

Japchae is a truly icon meal--- a stir-fried noodle dish made with sweet potato starch noodles, vegetables, and often beef or pork. It offers a gentle blend of textures and flavors. Korean street food is also incredibly popular. Tteokbokki, spicy rice cakes, and hotteok, sweet filled pancakes, are just a couple of examples that captivate locals and visitors alike. I'm waiting on the day that I can travel to south Korea to try the street food!
